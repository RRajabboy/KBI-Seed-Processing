Our Grain Cleaning

We clean to human consumption standards

Contact us to hear more about how we can clean your product to meet human consumption standards. We want to help you get the most of your product!

Seed Cleaning

We are a licensed seed cleaning operation for both conventional and organic seed. We work to ensure that we are meeting a high purity standard while minimizing product loss. You have worked hard to produce your seed and we want to help ensure that you are getting the most out of it in this upcoming growing season. We clean a variety of seed types including wheat, barley, oats, flax, peas, canary seed and more!

Upgrading

Our processing facility has been designed to be very adaptable. This allows us to be able to upgrade grain in a variety of ways, ensuring that you are getting the most out of your product based on the specific market you are selling to. So whether you are trying to sell into a niche market with strict purity requirements or are looking to decrease your dockage penalty, we would be happy to speak with you about what we can offer.

Product Seperation

We are able to separate many products with a high level of accuracy to ensure that your product loss is minimal and purity standards are met. With this service, intercropping is a great option and many growers are seeing the value in it. And, although all growers hope to never have it happen, on occasion multiple products get dumped into the same bin. We can help fix the mistake by separating back out your products!

We are expanding!

We are currently undergoing an expansion that focuses on the addition of a Buhler Colour Sorter. The colour sorter is cutting edge technology that will allow us to sort by shape, size, colour and texture. The addition of a sorter will allow us to expand on the services we already provide. The sorter cleans with great precision, resulting in a very high product purity rating and a low product waste.Our expansion also includes the addition of a bagging line, warehouse storage and a loading dock. This will be great for our customers, both current and new, looking to have their products shipped in dry vans or intermodal containers.
